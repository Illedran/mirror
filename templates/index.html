Hello there!

What is this? This website aims to improve your university life experience. Here you can find notes, exams, and teaching material created or gathered by other students before you. Key resources that these great women and men shared with all of us, asking nothing for their efforts and time. As first user of this service we want to thank them all from our hearts.

We wish to invite you to join this dynamic community exploiting the knowledge of our artifacts and participating in the creation and sharing of new ones.
